Description: A foundational standing pose. Strengthens the legs and core.

Benefits: Improves posture, increases awareness.

Instructions: Stand tall with feet together, engage your core, and lift your chest.

Common Mistakes: Arching the back, forgetting to breathe.

Posture Tips: Keep your shoulders relaxed and your gaze forward.
